My Development Sandbox

by Roo da Silva

Hi, my name is Roo, director of KEEN, an awesome non-profit in the UK. I am lucky to get to work everyday on solving problems and creating things as part of a fantastic team, in a fab community, and with amazing partners and collaborators.

Digital plays an increasingly central role in our work, and it is easy to feel out of the loop when you do not understand things, even if just a bit. This 'sandbox' is my attempt to understand more about what our developers do, through learning a bit myself. Hopefully - as a result - I will be in a better position to support them, and truly appreciate their work!
