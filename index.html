My Development Sandbox

by Roo da Silva

Hi, my name is Roo, Director of KEEN, an awesome non-profit in the UK. I am lucky enough to work everyday on solving problems and creating things as part of a fantastic team, in a wonderful community, and with inspiring partners and collaborators.

Digital plays an increasingly central role in our work, and it is easy to feel out of the loop when you do not understand things! This sandbox is my attempt to understand more about what our developers do, by learning a bit myself, and hopefully as a result being in a better position to support them.
