Who am I?

Hi, my name is Roo, Director of an awesome non-profit in the UK - KEEN. I get to solve fun problems and create things every day, for work! I'm part of a fantastic team and community of partners and collaborators.

Digital tech plays a central role in our work. I have found that it is easy to feel out of the loop when you do not have sufficient understanding of how things work. This online 'sandbox' is my attempt to learn more about what our developers do by getting practical myself. Hopefully - as a result - I will be in a better position to support them, and truly appreciate their work!
